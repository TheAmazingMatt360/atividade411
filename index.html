A VERY SILLY GAME CALLED...

The Flyswatter Bros. is a CHAOTIC arcade game were you need to kill A LOT of PESTS to protect your ROAST CHICKEN! Every wave have 1:30 minutes, and, at the end of eache of every even wave can have a BONUS GAME or a BREAKTIME. If any pest touches the roast chicken... DAMN! You LOSE!!!

On the game, you play as the twins Billy and Boris, two brothers that use a FLYSWATTER as a weapon. Their mission is protect the roast chicken of a pest infestation, caused by the smell of the chicken.
